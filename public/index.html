About Us

TC TedCon Outsourcing Corp. is a fresh Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) company established in November of 2021. We are a subsidiary company of Timeline Manpower Services Corp. – a human resources and manpower business operating since 2008.

Mission & Vision

At TedCon, our mission is to provide the best outsourcing experience for our clients through the promotion of a culture of excellence within the company – by providing proper training and advancement of employees together with adequate tools and systems to ensure smooth operation and seamless integration.

With this at our core, our vision is to establish long term mutually beneficial partnerships with clients through sustained quality and effective service allowing for qualified and deserving individuals the opportunity to thrive thus becoming the leading name in outsourcing inthe Philippines.
